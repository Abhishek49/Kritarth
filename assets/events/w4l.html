Description:

Everybody has a different take on people’s life. While others pass, writers write because what actions fail to achieve, the words can take charge and inspire millions. Words For Life is such an event that, through different people’s tale, inspires you to live and celebrate life.

Prize: ₹2,000

Instructions:

Submit your micro tale to us at: online@kritarth.org before 22nd September, midnight.

Some important instructions to follow :

  • The participants have to submit their micro tales based on keywords 'root' or 'space', also bringing out the essence of inspiration or celebration of life. The character limit of the micro tales is 140 characters.
  • The participants will be sending their micro tales with the subject 'TALES FOR KRITARTH' along with the following details :-
    Name
    Year/Class of Study
    College/University
    Keyword Used
  • If any foul and vulgar usage of words is found or if the tale doesn’t carry the essence, it will be disqualified.
  • The tales will be uploaded on Kritarth’s Facebook page. The entry receiving maximum likes wins the event.

Description:

Over the years, KIIT has produced a top-notch line of alumni who have represented our University in both national as well international scale. Pratik Dash is such an eminent personality who has given this University of humble origins, a reason to be proud of. Pratik has achieved a series of accolades by virtue of his sheer talent and hard work, within a very short period of time. He had sung twice in the world reputed Grammy Awards, 2006-07. He has worked as a backup singer for the likes of international music icons, Kanye West and Alicia Keys. He has successfully sold seven original compositions for advertising placement. He has even been the lead singer for various bands like One Week Notice and Raaga. All these accomplishments make him stand out as a notable KIITian. He will be gracing us with his presence on the event "Rock Band" organised by Kritarth.
See you all there!

Venue: The same University Auditorium, Campus 6
Time: As soon the sun sets, 5 PM sharp.

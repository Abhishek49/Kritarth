National Service Scheme (NSS), under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports Govt. of India, focuses on the development of personality of students through community service. KIMAYA, in association with NSS brings forth BLOOD KONNECT, a voluntary Blood Donation Programme, whereby you can bestow life on the debilitated by registering yourself as a donor and being a part of this noble cause. Blood collected from all registered donors will be provided to an acclaimed Blood Bank, to be made available in any situation demanding an immediate requirement of blood. On 24th September, 2016, join us in our endeavour to lend a hand to all in need. Because it's not just blood. It's Liquid Life.
